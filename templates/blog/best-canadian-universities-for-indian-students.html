Best Canadian Universities for Indian Students in 2025: TOP TEN

If you're looking for the best Canadian universities for Indian students, Abroadin has got your back! 1. University of Toronto, Top Universities for Indian Students in Canada

  • Official Website
  • QS World University Ranking: #25
  • International Students: over 29,000

The University of Toronto is famous for its exceptional academic reputation, consistently ranked as the top university in Canada and among the best globally.

This university has a competitive acceptance rate of 43%, which shows a commitment to maintaining high academic standards. For Indian students whose English language is perfect, acceptance rates can reach up to 60% for some programs.

Admission to the University of Toronto is highly selective, with a minimum GPA requirement of 3.7 (93%) for most undergraduate programs, and a suggested GPA of 3.8 (96%) for more competitive courses.

The university offers over 700 undergraduate and 200 postgraduate courses, providing a diverse range of academic opportunities. Tuition fees for Indian students are approximately INR 36 lakhs for undergraduate programs and INR 28 lakhs for postgraduate programs.

 

2. University of British Columbia, Best Canadian Universities for Indian Students

  • Official Website
  • QS World University Ranking: #38
  • International Students: over 20,000

Securing admission to UBC can be challenging, particularly for Indian students, given its esteemed global reputation. Historical data suggests that the acceptance rate for graduate students stands at approximately 50%.

If you are an Indian student looking to study at UBC, the admissions process is the same as other universities. You should fulfill all Columbia University admission requirements to secure enrollment.

At the University of British Columbia, the average GPA for admissions is around 3.6 on a 4.0 scale, equivalent to 91%. For graduate admissions, an aggregate between 65-76% is typically required. You can strengthen your application with a GMAT score of 650+ or a GRE score of 320+ is advisable.

Tuition fees for the 12-month full-time program for Indian students in 2025 are approximately INR 39 lakhs.

 

3. Concordia University Montreal, Best Universities in Canada for Indians

  • Official Website
  • QS World University Ranking: #415
  • International Students: over 11,000

Montreal is home to top institutions like McGill University, the University of Montreal, and Concordia University. The latest is one of the best Canadian universities for Indian students because of the diversity and the number of international students studying there.

Concordia University, with an acceptance rate of approximately 78.2%, offers a moderately simpler admissions process compared to other top universities in Canada.

This university provides a wide range of innovative programs and courses, which they claim offer a unique and rewarding educational experience, featuring small classes, accessible and award-winning professors, and an affordable education.

4. York University, Top Universities for Indian Students in Canada

  • Official Website
  • QS World University Ranking: #362
  • International Students: over 10,000

If you are an Indian undergraduate applicant looking forward to continuing your studies at York University, you should meet the admission criteria, including academic performance and supplemental documents, which generally make the process accessible.

To be considered for admission, prospective students must have completed at least one year of full-time study at an accredited institution outside Ontario, maintaining a minimum average of 3 on a 4-point scale or its equivalent.

York University offers a range of tuition fees for Indian students, typically between 19 to 24 lakhs INR. This variability reflects the different costs associated with various programs and course structures.

 

5. McGill University, Best Canadian Universities for Indian Students

  • Official Website
  • QS World University Ranking: #29
  • International Students: over 9,700

McGill University is famous for its academic standards and competitive admission process. For Indian students aspiring to join undergraduate programs, a high level of academic performance in Grades 10, 11, and mid-year 12 is required.

This university has an acceptance rate of 39%, which makes it highly selective, making it crucial for applicants to present a strong academic record and a well-rounded application to improve their chances of acceptance.

Admission criteria also emphasize the applicant’s academic record, requiring a GPA of 3.00/4.00 or 3.20/4.00 in the last two years of study, along with two letters of recommendation.

The basic tuition fee of McGill for 2025 is expected to be almost 25 lakhs INR, which shows a relatively increase comparing the current educational year for Indian students.

 

6. University of Alberta, Best Universities in Canada for Indians

  • Official Website
  • QS World University Ranking: #96
  • International Students: over 8,200

The University of Alberta (U of A) is a prestigious institution that attracts a diverse student body from across the globe, including elite students from India.

Admission to the university is highly competitive, with an acceptance rate of around 58%. Meeting the minimum requirements does not guarantee admission, as students must fulfill all conditions outlined in their offer letters to secure their place.

The tuition fee ranges from INR 30 to 42 lakhs per academic year at one of the best Canadian universities for Indian students. This fee is very competitive compared to peer institutions worldwide.

7. Simon Fraser University, Best Universities in Canada for Indians

  • Official Website
  • QS World University Ranking: #319
  • International Students: over 7,500

Simon Fraser University (SFU) stands out as one of Canada’s premier research-intensive institutions, renowned globally for its contributions to research, knowledge development, and sustainability.

However, prospective students should be mindful of the financial commitment required, as tuition fees can be quite high comparing to peer institutions worldwide.

For Indian students, SFU provides multiple pathways for admission. You can apply directly to the university or through Fraser International College (FIC), an on-campus partner institution that offers a pathway program designed to support international students.

With an acceptance rate of 59%, the university is selective, requiring applicants to have a GPA between 2.40 and 3.20. This selectivity underscores the importance of academic excellence and preparation for those aspiring to join SFU’s esteemed academic community.

8. University of Windsor, Best Canadian Universities for Indian Students

  • Official Website
  • QS World University Ranking: #547
  • International Students: over 6,000

The University of Windsor is famous for its culturally diverse environment and offers a welcoming community that enhances the overall academic journey.

Undergraduate tuition fees at the University of Windsor range between 13 to 15 lakhs INR, while graduate students can expect to pay between 17 to 20 lakhs INR.

With an acceptance rate of around 70% for international students, the University of Windsor is relatively accessible compared to other institutions for Indian students.

However, applicants must meet specific criteria, including English proficiency test scores from exams such as IELTS, TOEFL, or PTE, and tests like GMAT and GRE.

 

9. Dalhousie University, Top Universities for Indian Students in Canada

  • Official Website
  • QS World University Ranking: #275
  • International Students: over 4,600

Dalhousie University needs a final senior year average of ‘B’ as the minimum requirement for considering applications, but many programs are highly competitive, demanding even higher academic achievements.

The university’s focus on impactful research and high-quality education ensures that its graduates are well-prepared to address complex challenges and drive positive change in their respective fields.

It also boasts top ten rankings in Geology, Pharmacy & Pharmacology, Biological Sciences, Law, Agriculture & Forestry, Environmental Sciences, and Medicine, reflecting its broad academic excellence.

 

10. University of Regina, Best Universities in Canada for Indians

  • Official Website
  • QS World University Ranking: #951-1000
  • International Students: over 3,000

The University of Regina (U of R) needs at least a 70% graduating average from a four-year baccalaureate degree for most programs, and a slightly higher threshold of 75% for English, History, Mathematics, Statistics, and the Master of Public Administration.

U of R offers a secure and affordable place to study, with a range of support services tailored to the unique needs of Indian students, fostering a sense of community and belonging.

The university’s moderate selectivity, with an acceptance rate of 70%, reflects its dedication to maintaining a high standard of education while being accessible to a diverse student body.

This balance is further exemplified by the impressive 90% visa success rate for international students, underscoring the institution’s commitment to facilitating a smooth transition for students from around the globe.
